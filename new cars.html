Mahindra Thar ROXX

The Mahindra Thar Roxx is a 5-door version of the Thar, offering not only two additional doors but also a suite of premium features, different styling elements, and powerful turbo-petrol and diesel engine options. The Thar Roxx is a lifestyle off-road SUV available with an optional 4-wheel-drive (4WD) but only with the diesel engine.
